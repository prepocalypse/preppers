about

Prepping is a philosophy, a movement born in the USA that aims to develop and implement strategies capable of guaranteeing safety and comfort during emergency situations. The Prepper - literally "the one who prepares" or "the one who is ready" - finds the main solutions to act in protecting himself and his family in case of some doomsday scenarios. The tools he uses are mainly sources of information, with updates on the riskiest events in his area. But also knowledge in terms of survival, such as the techniques for preparing and organizing his own material. There are essential items inside every kit of every Prepper. At the same time, everyone knows their personal needs and strategies.

If your world ended tomorrow, would you be ready?

Preppers call it TEOTWAWKI: the end of the world as we know it. It feels like an apocalypse and actually it is so.
Many phenomena lead us to reason about the reality of an apocalypse: earthquakes, fires, social disorders, nuclear attacks or even cyber.
Being a prepper means anticipating the events considering their risks, needs and solution to act during an emergency.
